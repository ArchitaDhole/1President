Our Inspiration
Shri. Prakash P. Khandare
(President of Akurdi Chinchwad Education Society)

It is my pleasure and honor to write to you in my capacity as a president having been involved with Dr. D. Y. Patil English Medium School & Jr. College (Maharashtra State Board) for over 12 years and 7 years with New Pune Public School (CBSE Curriculum). It is my immense pleasure to announce that 50 glorious years have been successfully completed by this society rendering service in the field of education. Hence my core concern is to work toward attaining high ethical standards in education with an emphasis on the values of life. My work will be in the harmony with the student’s aspirations. I believe in freedom of expression independent thinking and positive personal values & qualities for maintaining a sense of responsibility towards society through respect & coordination. I strive to enhance each student’s self-worth, confidence & pride by providing curricular programmes & activities that encourage participation in multinational experience to make most of the advantages offered by our small community. Our school campus is situated in the prime area of Pradhikaran, Nigdi and it is easily accessible to Railway Station and Bus Stop. We try to make our school and college a secure place and a training ground for the future development & also to prepare the students, best citizens of the country.
